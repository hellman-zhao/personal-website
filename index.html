Software Engineer

Last summer, I interned as a software engineer at FinePrintData, a startup financial newsletter company. I designed algorithms and predictive models using Python that allowed us to extract key investment data from public financial disclosures of members from Congress. Collaborating with the data journalism team, we were able to make this information easy to access, search, and comprehend to the general public. It was my participation in these projects that inspired me to continue pursuing computer science and a full-time software development position in the future!

Research Assistant

Also last summer, I did paleontological research with Professor Steve Wang on behalf of the Swarthmore College Mathematics and Statistics Department on the project “Estimating the Duration of a Mass Extinction.” Our goal was to develop a model that could predict how long it took for related groups of extinct species to go extinct and whether that duration of the extinction can be categorized as “simultaneous” or “gradual”, using recorded fossil occurrence sightings as our main source of data. Through repeated testing and modification of our algorithm using RStudio, we were finally able to produce and present three models for three different categories of data by the end of our research.
